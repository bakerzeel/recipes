Recipe
100% flour
6 % fresh yeast*
1.8 % salt
60 % water
5.2 % dextrose
5.0 % whey powder
5.0 % milk powder
3.0 % margarine

Filling
50 % raisins
* or instant yeast 1/3 of fresh yeast

Method
Mix the ingredients to a well developed dough.
Mix in the filling directly after mixing.
Dough temperature 26°C.
Scaling weight 450 gram.
Intermediate proof 25 minutes.
Mould the dough and put in tins.
Final proof 60 minutes.
Baking temperature 220°C.
Baking time 30 minutes.
